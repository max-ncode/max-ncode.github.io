Cookie Policy

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to the owners of the site.

How We Use Cookies

Purple Parrot uses minimal cookies to ensure our website functions properly and to improve your experience. We believe in privacy - for your children and for you.

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off. They are usually only set in response to actions you take, such as setting your privacy preferences or filling in forms.
  • Preference Cookies: These cookies remember your cookie consent choice so you don't have to set it every time you visit.

Analytics Cookies

With your consent, we use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. This helps us improve our content and user experience. Google Analytics collects:

  • Pages you visit and time spent
  • How you arrived at our site
  • Your general geographic location (country/city level)
  • Device and browser type

This data is aggregated and anonymous. We do not use it to identify individuals. Analytics cookies are only loaded after you accept our cookie banner.

What We Don't Do

In keeping with our privacy-first philosophy:

  • We do not use advertising or remarketing cookies
  • We do not share any data with third parties for marketing purposes
  • We do not build personal profiles about your browsing activity
  • We do not sell any data

Third-Party Services

Our contact form uses Formspree to process submissions. When you submit a form, your data is processed according to Formspree's Privacy Policy.

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in various ways. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies are stored and delete them individually
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block cookies from specific sites
  • Block all cookies
  • Delete all cookies when you close your browser

Please note that blocking all cookies may affect your experience on our website.

Your Consent

By clicking "Accept" on our cookie banner, you consent to our use of essential and preference cookies as described above. You can withdraw your consent at any time by clearing your browser cookies.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our use of cookies, please contact us at info@ncode.london.
