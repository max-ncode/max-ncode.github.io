About Purple Parrot

A European company with a simple philosophy: Safer Parenting. Perfectly Private.

Our Philosophy

We believe parents shouldn't have to choose between smart technology and their family's privacy. That's why we develop revolutionary algorithmic solutions that empower parents with comprehensive, real-time insights into their child's well-being - entirely offline.

We are dedicated to pioneering responsible technology that delivers peace of mind without compromise. We sit at the intersection of privacy and safety, proving that you can have both.

Made By Parents

When we researched monitors for our first child, we were alarmed at what we discovered. The devices meant to protect our baby were harvesting intimate family moments - bedtime stories, playtime, conversations - and streaming them to servers around the world.

We built Purple Parrot because we knew there had to be a way to have smart features without the stranger danger. Our daughter inspired us to create something better, and now we're sharing it with families everywhere.

With backgrounds spanning CERN, the European Space Agency, and satellite engineering, we brought aerospace-level engineering to solve one of parenting's most overlooked problems.

What We Stand For

Privacy First

Data that never leaves your home can never be breached, sold, or misused. We don't just protect your privacy - we make surveillance impossible by design.

European Values

Built in Estonia, backed by European tech leaders. We're inherently GDPR compliant because there's no data to regulate - it stays with you.

Purpose-Driven

We're not building technology for its own sake. Every feature we develop serves one goal: helping parents protect and care for their children.

Transparent

No hidden data collection, no complex privacy policies to decipher. Our technology is straightforward because your peace of mind should be too.
