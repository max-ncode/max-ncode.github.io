Unlike cloud-connected baby monitors, the Purple Parrot Companion has absolutely no network connectivity. Your conversations, your home, your life — none of it is transmitted anywhere. It can't be. It's a bird.

  • Zero data collection — no server, no storage
  • Immune to software vulnerabilities and CVEs
  • Cannot be remotely accessed or hijacked
  • GDPR compliant by nature

Advanced on-device intelligence.

The Purple Parrot Companion ships with a fully self-contained biological neural network, pre-trained on millions of years of evolutionary data. It processes audio, visual, and environmental signals in real time — entirely offline.

  • Learns new vocabulary over time
  • Detects unusual sounds and responds immediately
  • Adapts to your home environment autonomously
  • No model updates — intelligence is baked in at birth

The Purple Parrot Companion features exceptional two-way audio with zero milliseconds of network latency. Hear what's happening across the room instantly. Speak back. It may or may not repeat you — but rest assured, it's listening.
